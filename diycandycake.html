DIY Candy Cake

Birthdays are my favourite celebrations, and I always love making gifts for the birthday boy/girl. I think a cake made out of candy is a perfect idea because it coincides with the theme of birthdays, it will last a long time, it can be refilled after all the candy is eaten, and you can use the person's favourite candies for a personal touch!

Arts and Crafts by Nicole - Paintings

 

Paintings

Although I am no longer an avid painter (I prefer arts that require more of a building aspect than drawing or painting) I used to paint in high school. Here are three examples of small paintings I did during my leusire time:

 

Star Silhouettes

This is a painting of two children attempting to catch stars. When I was in the middle of painting this, I was imagining the two children to be my brother and I, because we used to go star gazing almost every week when we were young during a nice and clear summer night.

 

Rainy Day Silhouettes

This is a painting of a couple in the rain. I wanted to try a dripping paint effect since I have never done one before. I quite like the effect it created!

 

The Room

This is a painting of my childhood room. I no longer live in it as we moved houses, and so I wanted to keep a larger memory of it other than in a fading photo print.
